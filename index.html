BLOK

THE NATIVE TOKEN OF UNIVERSIA

EARNING WITH BLOK

Universians will be able to buy and sell BLOK via both Decentalized and Centralised exchanges. All BLOK from initial real estate purchases will be publicity burnt decreasing the overall supply of the token. BLOK will also be required to purchase items from within Universia for example; upgrading real estate space or purchasing items from within the marketplace. This is what makes BLOK valuable and necessary for the ecosystem to operate.

REBLOK

BUY, SELL & LEASE VIRTUAL REAL ESTATE

EARNING WITH REBLOK

Within Universia the purchase of real estate is known as Reblok. Universians will be able to purchase real estate following the BLOK token distribution event (TGE). The real estate will be a valuable commodity within Universia allowing Universians to speculate on the property and either resell for profit or lease for passive income opportunities.

ADBLOK

RECEIVE INCOME VIA ADVERTISING

EARNING WITH ADBLOK

Within Universia one of the key earning opportunities to investors is via advertising. Universia will work with key brand partners and give them a unique opportunity to advertise within Universia. A proportion of these advertising fees generated will be returned to Universia token holders.

token Lock

Universia real estate will be sold in a eight phase sale. All BLOK tokens used for the purchase of the BLOK real estate will be publicly locked reducing the supply of BLOK tokens.

UNI is the native token of Universia. UNI will be required to purchase or lease all real estate within Universia.
Universians will be able to purchase items from the Universia marketplace, via a NFT mechansim. The marketplace will showcase items to personalise both real estate and customise avatars.
BLOK is a utility token and offers holders a wide range of features within Bloktopia. Examples include, accessing exclusive events, joining pre sales and staking.
Universia will work on a Governance model. Reblok NFT holders will form part of that Governance council. Each NFT will vote via a DAO. Proposals will be made to the DAO where NFT holders will vote with the majority vote winning.
